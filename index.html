URY Computing Team

Hi there! This is the github presence of the computing team at University Radio York. We like computers and build stuff, and sometimes put it on github. That being this place.

What we do

  • Look after the URY website (bits of which are on our github!)
  • Maintain the URY streams and jukebox systems
  • Write our own presenter support programs and other fun tools
  • Other stuff that doesn't pertain to software

Getting involved

If you are a student at the University of York and would like to work with us, we would like to know. Email us at (name of our team, one word, starts with a c!)@ury.org.uk. Be aware that in order to have full system access you must be elected as a society officer and thus be a paid member of URY.
